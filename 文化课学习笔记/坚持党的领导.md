当 $a < 0$ 时：
$$
\begin{aligned}
\dfrac{-a - \sqrt{a(a - 1)}}{a} & = -1 - \dfrac{\sqrt{a(a - 1)}}{a}\\
& = -1 - \sqrt{\dfrac{a(a - 1)}{a^2}}\\
& = -1 - \sqrt{\dfrac{a^2 - a}{a^2}}\\
& = -1 - \sqrt{1 - \dfrac 1 a}
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
-1 - \sqrt{1 - \dfrac 1 a} & = -1 - \sqrt{\dfrac{a - 1}{a}}\\
& = -1 - \sqrt{\dfrac{a(a - 1)}{a^2}}\\
& = -1 - \dfrac{\sqrt{a(a - 1)}}{-a}\\
& = -1 + \dfrac{\sqrt{a(a - 1)}}{a}\\
& = \dfrac{-a + \sqrt{a(a - 1)}}{a}
\end{aligned}
$$
